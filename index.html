Welcome to Mallorca10

A Private Golf Society on the Beautiful Island of Mallorca

Mallorca10 is an exclusive golf society, based at Son Gual, bringing together passionate golfers for competitive yet friendly rounds.

The league follows the CONGU handicap system, ensuring fair competition across all skill levels. Each player's best 10 rounds count towards the final standings, rewarding consistency and improvement throughout the season.
